Structure of the study:

During this study, you will answer a questionnaire which will take about 20 minutes. Unlike what you may be used to not only your own decisions but also the decisions of other participants might determine your earnings. Your total earnings will be a fixed payment of 4 USD for answering the questionnaire plus potentially an additional payment depending on another participants' decision that may vary between 0 and 10 USD.

You will receive your earnings within ten days after completion of the study via your Prolific account.
